Now in early testing.

Mission

One thing, wholeheartedly.

caidal exists for a single reason: to help you get the most out of every visit with your doctor.
Medical visits are short, stressful, and easy to forget — and the important details slip through the cracks between appointments and between providers. We're devoted to closing that gap. To helping you walk in prepared, understand what actually happened, leave with clear next steps, and carry your full story into the next visit.
Always on your side of the table.

Value

What caidal gives you.

Walk in prepared.

Turn a vague worry into a clear plan — a focused summary of your situation, the evidence that backs it up, and the questions worth asking. No more remembering what you meant to say on the drive home.

Understand what's happening.

Capture the visit, make sense of the jargon in plain language, and surface the things worth a second look — contradictions, gaps, and anything the after-visit summary got wrong.

Follow through.

Every visit becomes clear, prioritized next steps — and a simple plan you can actually act on, instead of a forgotten sheet of paper.

It remembers, so you don't have to.

Your visits for the same problem know about each other. Each new appointment is aware of the ones before it — real continuity of care, even across different doctors and months apart.

Grounded in your health, and yours alone.

Answers are built from your own records and history — not generic advice. Your information stays on your device by default, sharing is always your choice, and you can see and remove anything at any time.

Features

Visit Prep

A ready-to-go briefing for your appointment — your situation, supporting evidence from your data, and the questions to ask.

Capture the visit

Record and transcribe what happens, and get flagged on anything worth a closer look.

After-visit review

Compare the after-visit summary against what was actually said — catch mistakes, contradictions, and missing pieces before they matter.

Actions & plans

Your visit becomes prioritized next steps and a clear, timeline-based plan.

Continuity of care

New visits automatically draw on your earlier ones for the same health problem.

Your data, connected

Bring in clinical records from Apple Health, plus your own notes, measurements, and documents — all optional, all in your hands.

Personalized assistant

Ask questions and get grounded, personalized answers — with the sources it drew from, so you can trust and verify.

Private by design

On-device by default, opt-in sharing, and full control to review or delete — built for health information.
